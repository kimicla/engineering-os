# Vision

Engineering OS should help me spend more time making high-impact engineering decisions and less time managing information.

## Success looks like

- I can quickly understand what currently matters.
- I can separate real priorities from noise.
- I can turn messy project signals into clear decisions.
- I can review my work without reconstructing context from scratch.
- I can evolve my operating model without constantly redesigning it.

## Non-goals

- Build a perfect note-taking system.
- Capture every piece of knowledge.
- Optimize for beautiful documentation.
- Replace judgment with automation.
- Create process overhead that I cannot sustain.
